COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS POLICY
        
        1. SALARY STRUCTURE
        - Salaries are reviewed annually in January
        - Merit increases based on performance reviews
        - Average merit increase: 3-8% based on performance rating
        - Pay grades: Junior (1-2), Mid-level (3-4), Senior (5-6), Lead (7-8)
        
        2. PERFORMANCE BONUS
        - Annual performance bonus up to 20% of base salary
        - Based on individual performance (60%) and company performance (40%)
        - Paid in Q1 of following year
        - Employees must be employed at time of payout to receive bonus
        
        3. HEALTH INSURANCE
        - Comprehensive health insurance for employee and immediate family
        - Coverage includes medical, dental, and vision
        - Company covers 80% of premium costs
        - Coverage begins on first day of employment
        
        4. RETIREMENT BENEFITS
        - 401(k) retirement plan with company match
        - Company matches 50% of contributions up to 6% of salary
        - Immediate vesting for employee contributions
        - Company contributions vest over 4 years (25% per year)
        
        5. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
        - Annual learning budget: $2,000 per employee
        - Covers courses, certifications, conferences, books
        - Manager approval required for expenditures over $500
        - Tuition reimbursement available for degree programs (up to $5,000/year)
        
        6. WELLNESS BENEFITS
        - Gym membership reimbursement: up to $50/month
        - Mental health support: 6 free counseling sessions per year
        - Wellness days: 2 additional paid days off per year
        
        7. EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTIONS
        - Eligible employees receive stock options based on level and performance
        - 4-year vesting schedule with 1-year cliff
        - Exercise window: 90 days after termination
        
        8. OTHER BENEFITS
        - Life insurance: 2x annual salary
        - Disability insurance: 60% of salary coverage
        - Commuter benefits: Pre-tax transit passes
        - Free lunch on Wednesdays and Fridays
        - Employee referral bonus: $2,000 for successful hires
